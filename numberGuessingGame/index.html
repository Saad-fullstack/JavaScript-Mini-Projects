Number Guessing Game

Try to guess the secret number between 1 and 100.
You have 10 attempts to find the correct number.

How to Play

  • The computer will choose a random number between 1 and 100.
  • Enter your guess in the input field.
  • Click the Guess button.
  • If your guess is too high, you will see a message.
  • If your guess is too low, you will see a message.
  • If your guess is correct, you win.
  • You have only 10 attempts.
  • After the game ends, you can start a new game.
